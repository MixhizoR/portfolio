About Me

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Bozok University (GPA: 3.14) specializing in high-performance backend architectures. I obsess over optimization — whether it's reducing latency, eliminating race conditions, or designing resilient distributed systems.

My core stack revolves around Go (Gin/Fiber) and Java Spring Boot, backed by PostgreSQL, Redis, and RabbitMQ. I design systems that handle high concurrency gracefully and recover from failures automatically.
